About

I am a PhD student in statistics and machine learning jointly at Université de Montpellier, INRAE Montpellier and Universität Potsdam, working with my supervisors Joseph Salmon, Nicolas Verzelen and Alexandra Carpentier.

My research is about optimal detection and estimation for different statistical models related to Change-Point Detection in Times Series or Ranking in Crowd-Sourcing.
