Presentation

I am a PhD student working on statistics and machine learning jointly at Université de Montpellier and INRAE Montpellier. My research is about optimal detection and estimation for different statistical models related to Change-Point Detection in Times Series or Ranking in Crowd-Sourcing.

PhD supervisors:

  • Joseph Salmon: IMAG, Université de Montpellier

  • Nicolas Verzelen: INRAE Montpellier
